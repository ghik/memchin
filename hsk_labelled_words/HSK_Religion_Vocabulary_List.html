HSK Religion Vocabulary List

The following vocabulary list features vocabulary words from the HSK centered around the topic of Religion. In some cases the HSK vocabulary might be a bit lacking, and we may follow it will additional vocabulary we recommend for a fuller discussion on the topic.

Additional Religion Vocabulary

These words are not on the current official HSK list, but you may find them useful for your discussions on this topic.

Chinese Pinyin English HSK Level
基督教 Jīdūjiào Christianity; Christian B2
天主教 Tiānzhǔjiào Catholicism B2
佛教 Fójiào Buddhism; Buddhist B2
道教 Dàojiào Taoism/Daoism B2
印度教 Yìndùjiào Hinduism/Indian religion B2
伊斯兰教 Yīsīlánjiào Islam B2
东正教 Dōngzhèngjiào Eastern Orthodox Church B2
犹太教 Yóutàijiào Judaism B2
