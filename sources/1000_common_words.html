1000 Common Chinese Words For Beginners

May 9, 2025 / By Hao Vu

Just starting to learn Chinese and unsure where to begin? One of the most important steps to effective communication in Chinese is building a strong foundation of basic vocabulary. Understanding this need, Ni Hao Ma has compiled a list of 1000 common Chinese words, organized in a logical and easy-to-learn way, to help beginners gain confidence in using the language.

1000 Common Chinese Words

When you have a strong vocabulary foundation, listening, speaking, reading, and writing in Chinese become much easier and more natural. For beginners especially, mastering basic vocabulary is essential to quickly understanding lessons, communicating confidently, and making noticeable progress in your Chinese learning journey.
